Be Going To vs Will vs Present Continuous

BE GOING TO:

  • Plans/intentions already decided
  • Predictions based on evidence

WILL:

  • General predictions (opinions)
  • Instant decisions (deciding now)

PRESENT CONTINUOUS (future):

  • Specific arranged plans (time/place fixed)
I'm going to study English. (intention - general plan)
I'll help you. (instant decision)
I'm studying English at 5pm. (arranged - specific time)
