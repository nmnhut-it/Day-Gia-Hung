Comparative Adjectives

Use comparative adjectives to compare two people or things.

  • Short adjectives (1-2 syllables): add -er + than
  • Long adjectives (3+ syllables): use more + adjective + than
tall ‚Üí taller than
happy ‚Üí happier than
expensive ‚Üí more expensive than

Superlative Adjectives

Use superlative adjectives to compare one person or thing with a whole group.

  • Short adjectives: the + adjective + -est
  • Long adjectives: the most + adjective
tall ‚Üí the tallest
happy ‚Üí the happiest
expensive ‚Üí the most expensive

Too and Enough

TOO = more than necessary (negative meaning)
ENOUGH = sufficient amount (positive meaning)

Position: too + adjective, but adjective + enough

This coffee is too hot. (I can't drink it)
Are you tall enough to reach the shelf?
